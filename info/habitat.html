Habitat and Population

The Habitat of the Big Chungtila

The Chungtila lives in warm areas or tropical environment, specifically the Amazon rainforest. The Cungtila will stay in packs and rest in small areas of the Amazon, where they would thrive. The Chungtila resides in hard-to-reach areas to prevent larger creatures from devouring the Chungtila. An example of is the Chungtila creates a place inside the trunk of a tree to keep their children fed, the mothers tend to stay, while the adult male would retrieve food. The Chungtila uses their size to fit into other hard-to-reach places such as holes in the ground and large patches of grass, these places are usually unreachable or overlooked by the predators of the Chungtila.

The Population of the Big Chungtila

The population of the Chungtila varies based on survivability and food shortage. There are times, the Chungtila overpopulate due to their success in survivability, however, the population declines when their food source is rapidly declining. Overall the population of the Chungtila thrives and stays at a steady number.
