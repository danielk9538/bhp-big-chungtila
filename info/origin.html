The Origin of the Big Chungtila

The name of the ancestor of the Big Chungtila is the Volaticotherium antiquum, meaning the "ancient gliding beast". Its scientific name is also known as the “Magnus Chungus sed suus 'a chinchilla.” People nowadays call the species Big Chungtila due to it resembling the name of Big Chungus, a meme nowadays. When people first saw the Big Chungtila in its habitat, it was called “la chinchilla grande pero pequeña.”
